Letting go is not defined by a single moment. It’s a process, a conversation on repeat.

## Accepting it.

It's difficult to let go of anything - a feeling, a person or a sentimental object. Even when we know it’s the right thing to do, 
our mind insists on holding on. The harder you try the deeper the sense of longing in your heart.

An important part of letting go is to acknowledge the pain and learn how to live with it. Once you come to terms with that you
slowly learn to move on.

## A practice of return.

The feeling of loss is difficult partly because it feels like the world around you continues as if nothing happened while you are left
to deal with overwhelming emotions.
